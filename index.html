What do we mean by Active and Archived?

Active — the NDC a country currently operates under. This is their most recent, valid climate commitment.

Archived — an NDC that has been superseded by a newer submission. For example, a country's 1st generation NDC becomes archived once they submit a 2nd generation one.

When viewing a specific generation (e.g. 1st Generation), both active and archived NDCs from that round are included — because an archived NDC still represents a real commitment made during that cycle. Only 7 countries still have a 1st generation NDC as their latest active; the remaining 161 have moved on to newer generations.

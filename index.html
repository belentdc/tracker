About this chart

This chart shows the percentage of NDCs that include transport-related targets across three generations of submissions. Each bar represents 100% of NDCs submitted in that generation — 168 in the 1st, 148 in the 2nd, and 117 in the 3rd.

Use the Region filter to explore results by geographic area. The subtitle shows the exact number of NDCs for the selected region.

About the map

Green — country has a transport target in its latest active NDC.

Light blue — country included transport targets in a previous NDC generation but not in its latest active submission.

Grey — country has never included a transport target in any NDC.

White / outline — country has not submitted an NDC.

EU member states are shown collectively based on the EU NDC, which is submitted on behalf of all 27 members.

About this chart

This chart shows the number of NDCs mentioning each transport mitigation measure category. Use the generation toggles to compare how priorities have shifted over rounds. Multiple generations can be selected simultaneously.

The Region filter limits the analysis to countries in the selected geographic area.

What do we mean by Active and Archived?

Active — the NDC a country currently operates under.

Archived — superseded by a newer submission, but still represents the commitment made in that round.

When viewing a specific generation, both active and archived NDCs from that round are included.

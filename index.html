About this chart

This chart shows the percentage of NDCs that include transport-related targets across three generations of submissions. Each bar represents 100% of NDCs submitted in that generation. Use the Region filter to explore results by geographic area. The subtitle updates to show the exact number of NDCs for the selected region.

About the map

Transport target in latest active NDC The country's current operative NDC includes at least one transport-related target.

Transport target in a previous NDC The country included transport targets in an earlier NDC submission. That NDC is now archived, meaning it has been superseded by a newer generation that does not carry the transport commitment forward.

No transport target in any NDC The country has not included transport targets in any generation of NDC submissions.

No NDC submitted The country has not submitted an NDC to the UNFCCC.

What do Active and Archived mean?

An active NDC is the climate commitment a country currently operates under. An archived NDC has been superseded by a newer submission. It remains on record as evidence of past commitments but is no longer the operative document.

EU member states are displayed collectively based on the EU NDC, submitted on behalf of all 27 members.

About this chart

This chart shows the number of NDCs mentioning each transport mitigation measure category. Use the generation toggles to compare how priorities have shifted over rounds. Multiple generations can be selected simultaneously. The Region filter limits the analysis to countries in the selected geographic area.

About the map

Measure mentions by country The heat map shows how many times transport mitigation measures are mentioned in NDCs for the selected generation and categories. Darker colours indicate more mentions.

Generation selection Only one generation can be selected at a time on the map. Use the Reset button to return to the Latest Active view.

Category filter Select one or more measure categories to see how mentions are distributed geographically. When multiple categories are selected, the map shows the combined total of mentions. The country popup shows the breakdown per selected category.

Data note The map reflects mentions in submitted NDCs only. EU member states are displayed collectively based on the EU NDC, submitted on behalf of all 27 members.

What do Active and Archived mean?

An active NDC is the climate commitment a country currently operates under. An archived NDC has been superseded by a newer submission. It remains on record as evidence of past commitments but is no longer the operative document. When viewing a specific generation, both active and archived NDCs from that round are included.
